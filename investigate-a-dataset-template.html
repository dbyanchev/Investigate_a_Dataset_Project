OBSERVATIONS

1.Columns.
We will drop the columns named: id, imdb_id, homepage, tagline and overview. The reasons we justify below:

1.1 The two index columns ('id', 'imdb_id') can not contribute to our analisys
1.2 The 'homepage' column have ~8000 missing values in it.
1.3 The 'tagline' is just a slogan and we perfectly identify the movie by its title alone.
1.4.The 'overview' might help the eventual watcher, but not us.

2.Formats
We will need to convert columns to different format for our analysis.

2.1 We have complex numbers (scientific format) in the dataset, we will go for integers in the analysis.
2.2 Relase dates are not in format which is easy to analyse, we will aim for (YYYY-MM-DD) format.

3.Values
Some column values will need split, others we will replace with mean value.

3.1 Columns 'cast', 'director', 'genres' & 'production_companies' have multiple values and a separator.
We will need to separate them, if they are to be included in further analysis.
3.2 We have a lot of "0" values and NaN values in the dataset, they will be replaced with mean or dropped.
Zeros are especially present in the budget_adj and revenue_adj columns.

4.What will we analyze :

4.1 We will vizualise three trends in the movie industry with the data provided (raising movies released per year, raising popularity of the movies and raising adjusted profit).
4.2 We will find out what is the genre that has the most movies released ever, also what are the genres of the most popular movies for the last five years.
4.3 We will analyze Profitable Movies, by looking at runtime, genres, actors, directors and budgets of the most profitable movies in the database.

Conclusions¶

The Movie Industry is RED HOT.
Movies are more profitable, more popular and there are more and more of them released over the years.
Drama Movies are the ones released the most over the years, but in the recent five years Adventure and Science Fiction Movies are gaining popularity.
Overall movie popularity for the recent five years is raising extremy fast.
Same Actors / Directors are often behind the most of the profitable movies, for example : Steven Spielberg Directed 16 of the 397 most profitable movies.
Adventure genre is at the Top of "Most frequent Genre between the Highest Profitable Movies".
The average budget for a profitable movie is sitting arround 100 000 000 USD, by the time of the analysis.
